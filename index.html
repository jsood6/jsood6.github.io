Spark Adventure awaits!

Spark is an iOS app which boosts productivity and saves your time by helping you make a decision. Whether it's to choose a movie to watch or to go to eat at a restaurant, now with Spark, you can save that time for actually enjoying the activities. Less thinking and more doing!
